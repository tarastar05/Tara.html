Artist Bio

Tara Pirjaberi is San Jose based artist. Over the years she has experimented with photography, sculpture, ceramics, and glass blowing. Her work incorporates different mediums but she predominately works with oil on canvas. Pirjaberi lives with her two dogs, bird, and cat. Her love for animals and nature is what inspires her in her art. She has been drawing and painting from an early age. She has used her art as a way to express herself and as an escape from reality. Her style varies from realism to more abstract works. She is currenlty studying Studio Art at San Jose University and will graduate in the spring of 2021.
